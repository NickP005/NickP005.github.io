### üîß Troubleshooting

#### Connection Issues
1. Check that your server is running and accessible
2. Verify the server host and port in Settings
3. Ensure you have a stable internet connection
4. Try restarting the app

#### Account Issues
- **Forgot Password**: Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login screen
- **Account Deletion**: Go to Settings > Delete Account to permanently remove your account and data

#### Hardware Connection Issues
1. Verify your ESP/Arduino device is powered on
2. Check that the device is connected to the same network
3. Verify the device token is correct
